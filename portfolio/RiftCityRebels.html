Rift City Rebels

Rift City Rebels is a Platform fighter set in a near-future Winnipeg. It is the final group project in my college program, developed by 6 Developers and 12 Artists over the course of 4 months.
As this game is currently being developed, this page is still a work in progress.

Warden

Each programmer was paired with an artist to develop and design one of the playable fighters in the game. My character is Warden, a heavy brawler with enhanced strength and power over plants. The skull mask was inspired by folk horror, which influenced his history as a "boogeyman" of the game setting. His physique informed a heavy hitting playstyle with moves influenced by professional wrestling, with some more fantastical abilities added to showcase his plant based powers.

Dynamic Camera

One system I was in charge of developing was the in match camera. As the players are moving around the screen a lot, a system was developed to continually track their positions and calculate a center point for the camera to move towards. Constraints are set up so that the camera doesn't stray too far from the center of the stage when players are launched out of bounds. In addition to calculating the center point between all players, the camera also tracks the furthest distance between any two players, and uses that to determine how far it needs to zoom in or out to keep everyone on screen.
